Frequently Asked Questions

What is the EDU Platform?

EDU Platform is an online education platform offering a wide range of courses in technology, business, and creative fields. Whether you’re a beginner or a professional, we provide resources to help you achieve your learning and career goals.

How can I track my progress in a course?

You can track your progress directly from your dashboard. Each course has a progress bar showing how much of the course you’ve completed. Additionally, you’ll receive notifications when you reach key milestones.

Will I receive a certificate after completing a course?

Yes! All Medium and Pro plan courses include a certificate of completion that you can showcase on your LinkedIn profile, resume, or portfolio. Free courses do not offer certificates.

Will I get a reminder for upcoming deadlines or exams?

Yes, we send automatic email reminders for upcoming deadlines and exams. Additionally, you can view your course schedule, deadlines, and upcoming assessments directly in your dashboard.

Is there a way to customize my learning schedule?

Yes, our platform is flexible! You can learn at your own pace, and we offer personalized learning schedules. You can plan and adjust your study hours based on your personal preferences. Pro users can also access more advanced scheduling tools and reminders.
